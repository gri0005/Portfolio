The Very Pulse of the Machine
Re-designed sound and music by Jasmine Griffiths
Video scene from Love, Death + Robots

How to remove a video from its context, yet retain meaning? How to reinvent a soundtrack, yet stay true to the image? These are problems that must be solved when creating a reinterpretation of the sound of an existing work, and I did my best to tackle them in my edition of The Very Pulse of the Machine, from the Love, Death & Robots series of animations.
