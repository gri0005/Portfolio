Maribyrnong: A Walk Through Time
Sound and montage by Jasmine Griffiths

Over the course of the last two hundred years, the land and waters of the Maribyrnong River and its surrounding valley have been subjected to heavy degradation, pollution and mistreatment. The history of the location and the changes it has undergone over time provide a unique insight into the consequences of colonisation, industrialisation and urbanisation. Drawing on research about the changes the valley has undergone, this work guides the viewer through the history of the area, while remaining rooted in the present.
