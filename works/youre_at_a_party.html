You're At A Party
Writing, design, art and coding by Eamonn Harte
Music and sound design by Jasmine Griffiths

You're At A Party is a narrative game / piece of interactive fiction that explores the complexities of queer relationships. Unfolding over one night, at a party in a suburban sharehouse, the player comes face to face with their ex and his new partner, and has to navigate the tense, awkward situation that unfolds after that.
