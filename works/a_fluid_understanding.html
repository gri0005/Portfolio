A Fluid Understanding
Video by Jasmine Griffiths
Music by Kevin MacLeod

A Fluid Understanding is a multi-channel video work, which uses generative procedures to create immersive and mesmerising video art. It was created to be projected at a large scale on three walls of a room. The organic forms and dynamics are derived from practical experiments involving fluids and dyes. The material is then processed through software (TouchDesigner), at which stage digital generation of visuals occurs. A Fluid Understanding is an investigation into recursive generative processes through dual digital and organic methodologies.
